A Tribute to Subhas Chandra Bose

Birth January 23, 1897
Death August 18, 1945 (aged 48)
Known For Indian Nationalist, Founding member of Azad Hind Fauj (Indian National Army)
Role Leader of the Indian National Army

Subhas Chandra Bose played a key role in India's struggle for independence against British rule. He was known for his valiant efforts and determination to free India from colonial oppression.

He famously said, "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!" inspiring countless Indians to join the freedom movement.

Netaji demonstrated extraordinary commitment to the cause of India's freedom. His unparalleled courage and charisma inspired Indians to fight fearlessly against colonial rule. His powerful personality had a profound impact on our freedom struggle. The nation will always remember Netaji with utmost gratitude."
